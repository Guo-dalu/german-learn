# Food & Drink 🍎

Germans love good food! Here are the essential words for eating and drinking in German. Every noun has a gender — **der** (masculine), **die** (feminine), or **das** (neuter).

## Core Vocabulary

| German | Article | English | Gender |
|--------|---------|---------|--------|
| Wasser | das | water | neuter |
| Milch | die | milk | feminine |
| Brot | das | bread | neuter |
| Käse | der | cheese | masculine |
| Apfel | der | apple | masculine |
| Suppe | die | soup | feminine |
| Ei | das | egg | neuter |
| Fleisch | das | meat | neuter |

## Key Verbs

- **essen** — to eat
- **trinken** — to drink
- **kochen** — to cook
- **kaufen** — to buy
- **mögen** — to like

## Example Sentences

- Das **Wasser** ist kalt. *(The water is cold.)*
- Ich **esse** das **Brot** mit **Käse**. *(I eat the bread with cheese.)*
- Wir **trinken** **Milch** jeden Morgen. *(We drink milk every morning.)*
- Der **Apfel** ist **süß**. *(The apple is sweet.)*
- Sie **kocht** eine **Suppe**. *(She is cooking a soup.)*

## Tips for Remembering Gender

- **das** Wasser, **das** Brot, **das** Ei, **das** Fleisch → many food items are neuter
- **die** Milch, **die** Suppe → feminine nouns often end in -e or -ch
- **der** Käse, **der** Apfel → masculine for these classics!
