## Language Notes

- Most rooms are **das** (neuter): das Zimmer, das Schlafzimmer, das Badezimmer, das Wohnzimmer — "Zimmer" is always neuter.
- **die Küche**, **die Toilette**, **die Wohnung** — these three key home words are all feminine.
- **wohnen** vs **leben** — both mean "to live", but wohnen refers to where you physically reside: *Ich wohne in Berlin.*
- Compound nouns are common in home vocabulary — the gender always follows the last word: das Schlaf**zimmer**, die Bad**wanne**, der Kühl**schrank**.
