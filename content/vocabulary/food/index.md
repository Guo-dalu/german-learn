## Language Notes

- Most food items are **das** (neuter): das Brot, das Ei, das Fleisch — when unsure, guess neuter for food.
- **die** nouns often end in **-e** or **-ch**: die Milch, die Suppe, die Banane, die Orange.
- **kochen** vs **essen** vs **trinken** — learn these three verbs together, they cover most food situations.
- Plural of **das Ei → die Eier** is irregular and very common — worth memorizing.
