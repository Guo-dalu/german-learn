## Language Notes

- Weather verbs use **es** as an impersonal subject: **es regnet**, **es schneit**, **es ist sonnig** — you never name a different subject.
- **der** dominates weather nouns: der Regen, der Schnee, der Wind, der Nebel, der Frost — when unsure about a weather phenomenon, masculine is a strong default.
- **Jahreszeit** is feminine (**die**), but all four seasons are masculine: **der Frühling, der Sommer, der Herbst, der Winter**.
- **anziehen** (to put on clothes) is a separable verb: *Zieh deinen Mantel **an***.
