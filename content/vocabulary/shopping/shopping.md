## Language Notes

- **kosten** vs **bezahlen** vs **kaufen** — *kosten* is for price (*Was kostet das?*), *bezahlen* for the act of paying (*Ich bezahle mit Karte*), *kaufen* for buying (*Ich kaufe ein T-Shirt*). Don't swap them.
- Clothing sizes use **Größe**: *Ich trage Größe 38*. Note *tragen* (to wear/carry) is the correct verb for wearing clothes — not *anziehen*, which means to put on.
- **teuer** (expensive) and **billig** (cheap) are simple opposites, but Germans often use **günstig** (good value) instead of billig — billig can imply low quality.
- **der Euro** → plural **Euro** (not Euros) in prices: *Das kostet zwanzig Euro*, not *zwanzig Euros*.
